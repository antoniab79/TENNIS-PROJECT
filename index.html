Meet the PTC Team

Antonia Bezinovic

Antonia made a significant impact on the tennis world as a junior player from Croatia. She began her tennis journey at the age of 6 and quickly rose to prominence, earning the titles of Under 12 and Under 14 Champion of Croatia.

Her talent extended beyond her home country as she competed in prestigious junior tournaments across Europe and represented a German club in the Bundesliga. In 2008, Antonia qualified as an LTA level 3 coach, dedicating the past 15 years to sharing her expertise and passion for tennis.

Thomas Freeman

Thomas has been coaching for over 20 years, loves the physicality of tennis and the fitness involved in the sport. He never played professionally, but went through the educational route with his coaching. Thomas is an LTA level 4 certified coach, and heads up the coaching team at Thames Ditton lawn tennis club. He is hugely passionate about the sport and improving players.
